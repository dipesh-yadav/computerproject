Where Life Has Come To?

  The paragraph begins with the time: When we were just a little baby. the first day when we cried but our mother, father, whole family and relatives smiled. Then slowly we learnt walking holding our mom's hand. As we fell on the ground while walking, the ground got beaten from the mom and we got support. We again stood up and started walking again. Then after few yearswe started going to school, made new friends and smiled with new faces. But the time has made us grow so much that now we are in class 9. But actually it is going well as like that of junior classes. Oh sorry this is what we tell in-front of others. But no, should i tell the truth? Ok let me tell the truth. Actually there is nothing like before. Like each day we are piled up with a lot of pressures. Before when we were small, just a child, child who just played without any worries and pressure of homework and study, those were good old days.

  But now when we are in class 9 our day is just going with the routine: wakeup at 4 or 5, do homework, study, go school, in school give test, practicals, do classwork, get scolded by our teacher, get home work, come home, again start to do hw and then again learn for the next days test, and someday dont even dare to sleep else you will get scolding. There is so much of pressure that now we are just busy with all those writings and test all the time like literally have forgotten the meaning of life or the term "enjoyment in life". Yes, I know you guys maybe thinking then : How every day? There is a day called Saturday, the day on which you get holiday. Ok so it is just named as Saturday for us and we are told that on this day you get holiday and enjoy. but for us it is not holiday. When saturday comes we get even more homework saying, "what will u do whole day?".

  Literally this is the life right now we students are living.Each and every day, every minute we talk about the old days. We dive deep into those feelings of the old days and unknowingly fall tears remembering those. We again want to live that life. But then what can we do....life is not a car that has reverse gear. So each day holding these loads on our sprained shoulder we move on with so called smile on our face. We even dont have time to go and sit with our family and talk about something. There is no time to even refresh our mind because we just are in deep relationship with someone but sadly that someone is tests and worksheets.

  So what should we do in this mere reality of we student's life where we are just packed in the packet of pressures?

  Right now also while typing this I, the narrator, is thinking about my those days when I cried for the chocolates and when i climbed on to mom and father's back. Those were the true epitome of the life. Literally right now I am in the imagination of those days and am embraced with those feelings. Oh wow the tears fell!!

  ==> No this isn't a complain about the life that is going on right now . But it is the feeling that i m feeling right now and wanted to drop it on this page.
